Meghauli Serai A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park

Contact & Location

Meghauli Serai, A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park is located at the edge of Chitwan National Park in the Terai lowlands of Nepal. Our resort is easily accessed from Kathmandu, both by road (176 kms) or by flight. Transportation is available to and from the international and domestic airports in air-conditioned SUVs or minivans operated by skilled drivers. Please note that all road transfers can be booked on request with Taj Safaris or through your preferred Nepalese ground handler.

Arriving at the resort

By Road: The drive from Kathmandu to Meghauli (approximately 4.55 hours/ 176 kms) takes you through the middle hills of Nepal and along the Trishuli River, popular with the rafting community of Nepal.

By Air: A short 20 minute flight from Kathmandu to the Bharatpur Airport, followed by a 1 hour or 30 kms drive to the property.

By Private Charter:An alternative is to take a private charter from the Tribhuvan International Airport in Kathmandu to the Meghauli Airstrip which is a short 15-minute drive

By Helicopter:Landing site for chopper is available on site. Coordinates may be shared via request..
