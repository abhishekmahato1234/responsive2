Meghauli Serai A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park

GALLERY

Declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1984, Chitwan or “Heart of the Jungle” is located in the Terai lowlands of Nepal and world-renowned as one of the best wildlife-viewing national parks in Asia. Overlooking the park from the banks of the River Rapti, Meghauli Serai, A Taj Safari Chitwan National Park is a 5-star resort that seamlessly blends the breathtaking aura of its surroundings with contemporary luxury. Please view the images in our photo gallery to learn more about the experience awaiting you at this truly singular safari holiday getaway.
